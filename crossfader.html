Crossfader

Crossfader is the world’s simplest DJ app.

As the sole product designer, I work closely with the entire team to define the functionality and flow of new features, create and iterate on interaction and visual designs, then oversee (for iOS) and execute (on the web) the implementation of final designs.

After shipping updates, I synthesize user feedback and look at usage data to guide the next release.

The way we run product at Crossfader very lean and iterative. I also created the logo, branding, and visual design for everything Crossfader-related.

Crossfader is available in the app store.

Prototyping

Prototyping interactions was essential to our process as we tried to create the most intuitive and fluid mixing experience possible.

Onboarding

We found that the largest barrier to retention was that users never had the 'Ah-Hah' moment. This led us to overhaul the Crossfader tutorial, letting users learn the controls by interacting with the music.
