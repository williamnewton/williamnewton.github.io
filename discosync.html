DiscoSync

Created in 48 hours, discosync let you to capture live audio output and stream it through a server to our client, on the web and on iOS. Originally conceived as a ‘silent disco app’, it then became a ‘turntable.fm for live performance DJing’.

My role was designing the logo, designing and implementing the interfaces, animations, and putting together our presentation and pitch.

We won 'second place,' a $25k investment prize.
